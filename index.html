✨ 主な機能 ✨ Key Features

💊

服薬管理Medication Management

薬の登録、リマインダー通知、服薬記録。飲んだ・飲めなかった・後で通知の3つから選ぶだけ。写真も添付可能。Register medications, get reminders, and record intake. Simple 3-choice recording: taken, skipped, or remind later. Photo attachment supported.

🌡️

健康記録Health Records

体温、血圧、脈拍、体重、食事量、排泄、体調メモ。記録したい項目だけでOK。グラフで変化が見える。Temperature, blood pressure, pulse, weight, meals, excretion, and condition notes. Record only what you need. Track changes with graphs.

📅

カレンダーCalendar

通院、訪問看護、デイサービスなどの予定を一元管理。繰り返し設定や担当者アサインも可能。Manage hospital visits, home nursing, day services, and more in one place. Supports recurring events and assignees.

✅

タスク管理Task Management

「薬を受け取る」「シーツ交換」など、家族で分担。担当者を決めて、完了したら写真で報告。Share tasks like picking up prescriptions or changing sheets among family. Assign responsibilities and report completion with photos.

📝

メモ・申し送りCare Notes

気づいたことを写真付きで共有。タグ付け、コメント機能で家族間の申し送りに。Share observations with photos. Use tags and comments for seamless family communication and care handoffs.

👪

家族共有Family Sharing

家族メンバーを招待して全員で記録を共有。訪問看護師など外部スタッフの招待も対応。Invite family members to share all records. External staff (home nurses, etc.) can also be invited with limited permissions.
